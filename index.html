Viruses and Worms are malicious programs that self replicate. They exploit security vulnerabilities allowing the programs to spread among computers. Worm attacks are usual targeted at companies systems rather than personal devices. Worm attacks spread on their own and can appear through communication such as email with a link that will start the worm discretely. Viruses are very similar to worms but they need a corrupt system in order for it to be able to run. Viruses and Worms cost billions of dollars in damage every year and discreetly corrupt thousands of computers.
